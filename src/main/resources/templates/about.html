About Wishcloud

Welcome to Wishcloud – your personal wishlist in the sky!

We created Wishcloud with one simple mission: to make gift-giving easier, more joyful, and totally stress-free. Whether it’s for birthdays, Christmas, weddings, or just a little everyday surprise, Wishcloud helps you gather all your wishes in one place – and share them with the people who matter most.

Why Wishcloud?
We’ve all been there: standing with a gift, wondering “Will they actually like this?” With a Wishcloud list, there's no need to guess. You get clarity, inspiration, and the ability to reserve gifts – so everyone can enjoy giving and receiving with confidence.

It’s easy – and free
Creating a wishlist takes just a few minutes. Add wishes from anywhere online or write your own. Then share your list with friends and family, and let them reserve gifts to avoid duplicates and surprises that miss the mark.

Who's behind Wishcloud?
We're a small team of gift-loving humans who care deeply about connection, kindness, and a touch of tech magic. We believe gifts are more than just things – they’re moments, memories, and little acts of love.

Our vision
At Wishcloud, we dream of a world where it’s easier to make each other smile. Whether you're the planner, the last-minute shopper, or just want to avoid awkward gift swaps, Wishcloud is here for you.

Thanks for being here ️
We hope you enjoy Wishcloud – and that your wishes come true!
